The Indian Army

A Tribute to Our National Heroes

The Guardians of the Nation

Guardians of our nation, standing strong and tall,
Through trials and challenges, you never let us fall.
In valor and courage, you march day and night,
Protecting our land, in your unwavering might.
With selfless devotion, you shield us from harm,
In deserts and mountains, your duty is the charm.
Saluting your bravery, we stand proud and true,
Indian Army soldiers, we owe our thanks to you.
From border to border, your sacrifices unfold,
A story of resilience, in tales yet untold.
In the face of danger, you hold the line,
Indian Army soldiers, your courage shines.
So here's to your service, so noble and grand,
With gratitude and respect, we'll forever stand.
In the heart of the nation, your legacy will stay,
Indian Army soldiers, our heroes every day.
